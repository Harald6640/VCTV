# SHORT 01 — "Das Sofa-Problem" (25 Sek.)

> **Ziel:** Problem aufzeigen → Zielgruppe ansprechen
> **Plattform:** YouTube Shorts / TikTok / Instagram Reels (9:16, 1080x1920)
> **Ton:** Humorvoll, schnell, wiedererkennbar

---

## VIDEO-PROMPT (Sora / Runway / Pika)

```
Cinematic vertical smartphone advertisement video, 9:16 aspect ratio, 25 seconds.

SCENE 1 (0–4s):
A person sitting comfortably on a couch in a cozy living room, watching YouTube on a large TV. Warm ambient lighting. They laugh at the content on screen. Slow zoom in from medium shot to close-up. Realistic, warm color grading.

SCENE 2 (4–8s):
The same person reaches for the TV remote on the coffee table, fumbles with it awkwardly trying to navigate to "like" a video. Frustrated expression. Slightly comedic. Close-up on hands struggling with remote buttons.

SCENE 3 (8–13s):
They give up on the remote and grab their phone, but can't find the video on their phone that's playing on the TV. Scrolling frantically. Exaggerated confused expression. Warm orange side lighting.

SCENE 4 (13–18s):
FREEZE FRAME. White text appears over the frozen scene: "Kennst du das?" Bold, orange accent color (#FF6B00), DM Sans font. Subtle animated underline appears.

SCENE 5 (18–25s):
Quick cut to a clean white background. A hand holds an iPhone displaying the VoiceComment TV app — orange logo visible, large orange "Liken" and blue "Abonnieren" buttons clearly visible on screen. Text slides in from bottom: "VoiceComment TV — Dein Handy als YouTube-Fernbedienung". Orange color (#FF6B00). App logo pulses once. Fade to white.

Style: Realistic, cinematic, warm tones, smooth transitions, no shaky cam. Music: upbeat lo-fi beat, fades out at end.
```
